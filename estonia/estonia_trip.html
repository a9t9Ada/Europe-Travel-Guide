Trip Plan 1: Tallinn City Vibes (History + Culture)

Day 1 – Historic Heart of Tallinn
  •  Morning: Tallinn Old Town & Town Hall Square – cobblestone streets, medieval charm, and vibrant cafés
  •  Afternoon Alexander Nevsky Cathedral & Toompea Hill – panoramic views and stunning Orthodox architecture
  •  Evening: Traditional Estonian dinner – black bread, herring, and local specialties along the Old Town alleys
Day 2 – Museums & Stories
  •  Morning: Kumu Art Museum – modern and classical Estonian art, cultural insights
  •  Afternoon: Seaplane Harbour Museum – maritime history, interactive exhibits, and historic ships
  •  Evening: Evening walk in Kadriorg Park – elegant gardens, Baroque palace, and relaxed vibe
Day 3 – Relax & Local Life
  •  Morning: Telliskivi Creative City – street art, design shops, and cozy cafés
  •  Afternoon: Rotermann Quarter – modern architecture blended with industrial heritage
  •  Evening: Sunset by Tallinn Bay – walk along the waterfront and enjoy local pastries

Trip Plan 2: Lahemaa & Northern Estonia (Nature + Heritage)

Day 1 – Coastal Villages & Manor Houses
  •  Morning: Palmse & Sagadi Manor – historical estates, museums, and scenic gardens
  •  Afternoon: Altja Fishing Village & Lahemaa Coast – traditional coastal life, wooden houses, and calm waters
  •  Evening: Dinner at a local inn – fresh fish and regional flavors
Day 2 – National Park & Forest Trails
  •  Morning: Lahemaa National Park – hiking trails, pine forests, and bog landscapes
  •  Afternoon: Viru Bog Trail & Lookout Tower – panoramic views and serene nature
  •  Evening: Relax by the park lakes – local cafés or guesthouses with traditional cuisine
Day 3 – Waterfalls & Countryside
  •  Morning: Jägala Waterfall – Estonia’s widest waterfall, perfect for photos
  •  Afternoon: Rakvere & Väike-Maarja – small towns, castles, and countryside charm
  •  Evening: Evening local meal – hearty Estonian fare with warm hospitality

Trip Plan 3: Islands & Coastal Escape

Day 1–2 – Saaremaa Island
  •  Morning: Kuressaare Castle – medieval fortress with museums and gardens
  •  Afternoon: Kaali Meteor Crater – geological wonder and scenic walks
  •  Evening: Dinner by the harbor – local seafood and relaxed island vibes
Day 3 – Hiiumaa Island
  •  Morning: Kõpu Lighthouse – historic lighthouse with sea views
  •  Afternoon: Ristna & Tahkuna Coastal Walks – unspoiled beaches and nature trails
  •  Evening: Sunset over Baltic Sea – slow evening, island charm, and fresh seafood
Day 3 – Sweet Finale & Relax
  •  Morning: Kiluvõileib or pastries at a local café
  •  Afternoon: Beach walk or city sightseeing
  •  Evening: Sunset drinks & farewell dinner
