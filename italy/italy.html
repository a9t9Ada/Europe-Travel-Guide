Fun-Facts

Italy is home to the largest number of UNESCO World Heritage sites in the world, with over 55 locations recognized for their cultural and natural importance.

Pizza as we know it today was invented in Naples, but flatbreads with toppings have been enjoyed in Italy since ancient Roman times.

Italy produces more wine than any other country in the world, with regions like Tuscany, Piedmont, and Veneto leading the way.

The Colosseum in Rome could hold up to 50,000 spectators and hosted gladiatorial games, public spectacles, and mock naval battles.
