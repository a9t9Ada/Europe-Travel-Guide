Trip Plan 1: Zagreb City Vibes (History + Culture)

Day 1 – Historic Heart of Zagreb
  •  Morning: Ban Jelačić Square & Dolac Market – vibrant city center, local produce, and lively atmosphere
  •  Afternoon: Zagreb Cathedral & Tkalčićeva Street – Gothic architecture, charming cafés, and colorful streets
  •  Evening: Traditional Croatian dinner – taste štrukli or local meat dishes, soaking in city lights
Day 2 – Museums & Storytelling
  •  Morning: Mimara Museum or Museum of Broken Relationships – art, culture, and unique local stories
  •  Afternoon: Lotrščak Tower & Strossmayer Promenade – panoramic city views and historic vibes
  •  Evening: Modern dining in Zagreb – contemporary Croatian cuisine in cozy urban setting
Day 3 – Relax & Local Life
  •  Morning: Maksimir Park or Botanical Garden – peaceful strolls, greenery, and local life
  •  Afternoon: Medieval Upper Town (Gornji Grad) – cobblestone streets, small museums, and quiet corners
  •  Evening: Coffee & dessert at a café along Ilica Street – end the day slowly with local pastries

Trip Plan 2: Dalmatian Coast & Islands (Sea + Sun)

Day 1 – Split & Ancient Charm
  •  Morning: Diocletian’s Palace – Roman history, narrow alleys, and lively squares
  •  Afternoon: Riva Promenade – seaside stroll, cafés, and people-watching
  •  Evening: Dalmatian seafood dinner – fresh fish, olive oil, and sunset over the Adriatic
Day 2 – Island Escape
  •  Morning: Hvar or Brač – beaches, lavender fields, and crystal-clear waters
  •  Afternoon: Local villages & seaside walks – traditional houses, small taverns, and local crafts
  •  Evening: Sunset at a cliffside bar or beach – relax, sip local wine, and enjoy the view
Day 3 – Nature & Adventure
  •  Morning: Krka National Park – cascading waterfalls, walking trails, and swimming spots
  •  Afternoon: Šibenik Cathedral & Old Town – UNESCO charm, stone streets, and cultural atmosphere
  •  Evening: Dinner with coastal sunset – try local specialties like pašticada or grilled octopus

Trip Plan 3: Istria & Countryside Escape (Slow Travel)

Day 1–2 – Vineyards & Hills
  •  Morning: Motovun Hill Town – medieval streets, panoramic views, and truffle hunting vibe
  •  Afternoon: Istrian wineries & olive groves – tastings, nature walks, and picturesque scenery
  •  Evening: Local Istrian dinner – prosciutto, cheeses, and pasta specialties
Day 3 – Coastal Villages & Culture
  •  Morning: Rovinj Old Town – colorful houses, narrow streets, and Adriatic charm
  •  Afternoon: Poreč & Euphrasian Basilica – Byzantine architecture and seaside walks
  •  Evening: Sunset along the promenade – gelato, relaxed vibe, and golden light
Day 4–5 – Nature & Adventure
  •  Morning: Brijuni National Park – boat trip, island nature, and wildlife spotting
  •  Afternoon: Hiking, cycling, or kayaking – explore Istria’s inland trails and rivers
  •  Evening: Farewell dinner at a hilltop tavern – local flavors, wine, and sunset views
