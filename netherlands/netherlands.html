Food

Dutch cuisine is celebrated for its hearty flavors, simple ingredients, and comforting dishes. From fresh seafood and stroopwafels to traditional cheeses and stamppot, every meal tells a story of the Netherlands’ culture and traditions. Discover the tastes shaped by centuries of farming, fishing, and local craftsmanship that continue to delight both locals and travelers alike.

Fun-Facts

The Netherlands is home to over 10 UNESCO World Heritage sites, celebrating its history, architecture, and cultural achievements.
Dutch is a West Germanic language spoken by millions, reflecting the country’s unique linguistic heritage.
Amsterdam’s canal ring is a UNESCO World Heritage site, with over 100 km of canals and 1,500 historic bridges.
The Netherlands is famous for its tulip fields, producing billions of flowers every year, especially in spring.
Windmills are iconic symbols, with Kinderdijk boasting 19 perfectly preserved historic windmills.
The Dutch cycling culture is world-renowned, with more bikes than people and over 35,000 km of dedicated cycling paths.
