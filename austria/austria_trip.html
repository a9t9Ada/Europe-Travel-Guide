Plan 1: Vienna City Vibes (History + Culture)

Day 1 – Imperial Heart of Vienna
  •  Morning: Schönbrunn Palace – stroll through imperial halls and grand gardens, once home to the Habsburg dynasty
  •  Afternoon: St. Stephen’s Cathedral (Stephansdom) – admire Gothic architecture and climb the tower for city views
  •  Evening: Traditional Viennese dinner – Wiener Schnitzel and candlelit cafés in the historic center
Day 2 – Art, Music & Legacy
  •  Morning: Belvedere Palace – explore baroque elegance and Klimt’s The Kiss
  •  Afternoon: MuseumsQuartier – contemporary art, courtyards, and creative energy
  •  Evening: Classical concert or opera – experience Vienna’s world-famous music scene
Day 3 – Coffee & Calm
  •  Morning: Viennese coffee house culture – slow mornings with melange and Sachertorte
  •  Afternoon: Prater Park & Giant Ferris Wheel – green escape and panoramic views
  •  Evening: Danube Canal stroll – sunset lights, relaxed bars, and urban charm

Trip Plan 2: Salzburg & Alpine Charm (Music + Mountains)

Day 1 – Old Town Elegance
  •  Morning: Salzburg Old Town (Altstadt) – baroque streets, cathedral squares, and UNESCO charm
  •  Afternoon: Mozart’s Birthplace – discover the roots of a musical legend
  •  Evening: Fortress Hohensalzburg – golden-hour views over the city and Alps
Day 2 – Lakes & Sound of Music
  •  Morning: Salzkammergut Lake District – crystal-clear lakes and alpine villages
  •  Afternoon: St. Wolfgang or Hallstatt – postcard-perfect streets and lakeside walks
  •  Evening: Lakeside dinner – fresh fish, mountain air, and quiet reflections
Day 3 – Alpine Nature & Slow Travel
  • Morning: Cable car to Untersberg or Dachstein – panoramic mountain views
  •  Afternoon: Hiking trails or village cafés – slow travel through alpine scenery
  •  Evening: Traditional Austrian inn – hearty dishes and cozy atmosphere

Trip Plan 3: Tyrol & Nature Escape (Slow Travel)

Day 1–2 – Mountains & Fresh Air
  •  Morning: Innsbruck Old Town – colorful houses and alpine city vibes
  •  Afternoon: Nordkette Cable Car – dramatic peaks rising straight from the city
  •  Evening: Tyrolean dinner – dumplings, local cheese, and warm hospitality
Day 3 – Alpine Villages & Valleys
  •  Morning: Seefeld Plateau – open meadows, walking paths, and fresh mountain air
  •  Afternoon: Village walks & viewpoints – wooden chalets and peaceful scenery
  •  Evening: Sunset in the Alps – quiet moments and golden light
Day 4–5 – Lakes & Adventure
  •  Morning: Achensee Lake – turquoise waters surrounded by mountains
  •  Afternoon: Hiking, cycling, or boat ride – explore nature at your own pace
  •  Evening: Mountain lodge evening – stars above, silence around, pure alpine calm
