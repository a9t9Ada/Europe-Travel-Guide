Trip Plan 1: Sofia City Vibes (History + Culture)

Day 1 – Historic Heart of Sofia
  •  Morning: Alexander Nevsky Cathedral – explore Bulgaria’s most iconic landmark, golden domes, and grand interiors
  •  Afternoon: St. George Rotunda – walk through layers of Roman, Byzantine, and medieval history
  •  Evening: Traditional Bulgarian dinner – enjoy shopska salad, kavarma, and local wine in the city center
Day 2 – Stories of the Past
  •  Morning: National History Museum – discover Bulgaria’s rich heritage and ancient treasures
  •  Afternoon: Boyana Church – UNESCO-listed frescoes and spiritual atmosphere at the foot of Vitosha
  •  Evening: Modern Bulgarian dining – contemporary cuisine in a stylish yet cozy setting
Day 3 – Hills & Reflection
  •  Morning: Vitosha Mountain – cable car ride, fresh air, and panoramic views over Sofia
  •  Afternoon: Sofia Synagogue & Mineral Baths – cultural diversity and historic architecture
  •  Evening: City walk along Vitosha Boulevard – cafés, street life, and relaxed city charm

Trip Plan 2: Plovdiv & The Black Sea (Old Town + Coast)

Day 1 – Old Town Charm
  •  Morning: Plovdiv Old Town – cobblestone streets, colorful Revival houses, and timeless elegance
  •  Afternoon: Ancient Roman Theatre – dramatic setting with views over the city
  •  Evening: Kapana District – artsy quarter, local dining, and creative nightlife
Day 2 – Sea & Sun
  •  Morning: Varna or Burgas Beach – golden sands, gentle waves, and Black Sea breeze
  •  Afternoon: Seaside promenades – cafés, parks, and relaxed coastal walks
  •  Evening: Seafood dinner by the sea – fresh fish, local flavors, and sunset views
Day 3 – Nature & Relaxation
  •  Morning: Balchik Palace & Botanical Garden – elegant terraces overlooking the sea
  •  Afternoon: Wine tasting or spa time – local vineyards or seaside wellness
  •  Evening: Coastal night stroll – calm atmosphere and sea reflections

Trip Plan 3: Monasteries & Nature Escape (Slow Travel)

Day 1–2 – Mountains & Spiritual Heritage
  •  Morning: Rila Monastery – Bulgaria’s spiritual heart, frescoes, and mountain serenity
  •  Afternoon: Rila Mountains National Park – forest trails, fresh air, and peaceful walks
  •  Evening: Traditional mountain guesthouse – homemade food and quiet surroundings
Day 3 – Lakes & Legends
  •  Morning: Seven Rila Lakes – alpine hikes and breathtaking views
  •  Afternoon: Photography & nature breaks – turquoise waters and open landscapes
  •  Evening: Mountain sunset – silence, cool air, and golden light
Day 4–5 – Villages & Traditions  Morning: Koprivshtitsa – preserved Revival-era town with colorful houses  Afternoon: Local crafts & history – museums, folk traditions, and slow exploration  Evening: Farewell dinner – Bulgarian cuisine and traditional music
