Trip Plan 1: Classic Lisbon & Landmarks (3 Days)

Day 1 – Historic Lisbon:
  • Morning: Belém Tower & Jerónimos Monastery – explore Portugal’s Age of Discoveries
  • Afternoon: LX Factory – creative hub with cafés, shops, and street art
  • Evening: Alfama District – sunset views, narrow streets, and traditional Fado music
Day 2 – Hills, Views & Culture:
  • Morning: São Jorge Castle – panoramic city views over Lisbon
  • Afternoon: Baixa & Chiado – shopping, cafés, and historic squares
  • Evening: Prazeres do Bairro Alto – nightlife, wine bars, and local energy
Day 3 – Day Trip from Lisbon:
  • Morning: Sintra – Pena Palace & Moorish Castle
  • Afternoon: Quinta da Regaleira – gardens, tunnels, and hidden symbolism
  • Evening: Return to Lisbon – riverside dinner at Cais do Sodré

Trip Plan 2: Northern Portugal & Coastal Charm (4 Days)

Day 1 – Porto Arrival:
  • Morning: Ribeira District – colorful riverside streets
  • Afternoon: Dom Luís I Bridge & Gaia – scenic walks and views
  • Evening: Port wine tasting in Vila Nova de Gaia
Day 2 – Porto Culture & Wine:
  • Morning: Livraria Lello & São Bento Station – iconic architecture
  • Afternoon: Douro Valley – vineyard visit and wine tasting
  • Evening: Traditional Portuguese dinner with river views
Day 3 – Braga & Guimarães:
  • Morning: Braga Cathedral & Bom Jesus do Monte
  • Afternoon: Guimarães Historic Center – birthplace of Portugal
  • Evening: Return to Porto – relaxed evening stroll
Day 4 – Atlantic Coast:
  • Morning: Foz do Douro – ocean views and seaside walks
  • Afternoon: Seafood lunch by the Atlantic
  • Evening: Sunset at the coast & farewell dinner

Trip Plan 3: Portuguese Food & Culture Tour (3 Days)

Day 1 – Lisbon Flavors:
  • Morning: Pastéis de Nata & espresso at a traditional pastelaria
  • Afternoon: Time Out Market – local dishes and fresh seafood
  • Evening: Dinner with Fado music in Alfama
Day 2 – Cooking & Local Life:
  • Morning: Portuguese cooking class – bacalhau or seafood rice
  • Afternoon: Historic cafés & wine bars
  • Evening: Sunset at Miradouros – wine and city views
Day 3 – Coastal Tastes:
  • Morning: Day trip to Cascais – beach walk and fresh air
  • Afternoon: Seafood lunch – grilled sardines or octopus
  • Evening: Return to Lisbon – farewell dinner by the river
