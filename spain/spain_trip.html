Trip Plan 1: Classic Spain Highlights (4 Days)

Day 1 – Madrid Essentials Morning: Royal Palace of Madrid – explore royal halls and gardens Afternoon: Plaza Mayor & Puerta del Sol stroll Evening: Tapas tour in La Latina district
Day 2 – Art & Culture in Madrid
  • Morning: Prado Museum – masterpieces by Velázquez & Goya
  • Afternoon: Retiro Park – relax by the Crystal Palace
  • Evening: Flamenco show in a traditional tablao
Day 3 – Toledo Day Trip
  • Morning: Travel to Toledo – historic old town walk
  • Afternoon: Toledo Cathedral & Alcázar views
  • Evening: Return to Madrid & casual dinner
Day 4 – Barcelona Arrival
  • Morning: Travel to Barcelona
  • Afternoon: La Rambla & Gothic Quarter stroll
  • Evening: Dinner by the beach at Barceloneta

Trip Plan 2: Andalusia & Historic Spain (4 Days)

Day 1 – Seville Highlights
  • Morning: Seville Cathedral & La Giralda Tower
  • Afternoon: Alcázar of Seville – royal palaces and gardens
  • Evening: Walk through Triana & tapas dinner
Day 2 – Córdoba Day Trip
  • Morning: Travel to Córdoba
  • Afternoon: Mosque–Cathedral of Córdoba
  • Evening: Old town walk & return to Seville
Day 3 – Granada & Alhambra
  • Morning: Travel to Granada
  • Afternoon: Alhambra Palace & Generalife Gardens
  • Evening: Sunset at Mirador de San Nicolás
Day 4 – Relax & Local Life
  • Morning: Traditional breakfast & local markets
  • Afternoon: Free time for shopping or cafés
  • Evening: Farewell Andalusian dinner
