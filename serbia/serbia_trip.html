Trip Plan 1: Belgrade City Vibes (History + Nightlife)

Day 1 – Historic Heart of Belgrade
  • Morning: Kalemegdan Fortress – panoramic views where the Sava meets the Danube
  • Afternoon: Belgrade Fortress & Military Museum – layers of history and ancient walls
  • Evening: Skadarlija Street – traditional Serbian dinner, live music, and old-town charm
Day 2 – Stories & Culture
  • Morning: Nikola Tesla Museum – innovation, legacy, and science
  • Afternoon: Zemun Old Town – riverside walks, pastel houses, and cobblestone streets
  • Evening: Riverfront dinner – floating restaurants with city lights on the water
Day 3 – Relax & Urban Life
  • Morning: Ada Ciganlija – lakeside walks, cycling, and coffee by the water
  • Afternoon: Republic Square & Knez Mihailova – shopping, cafés, and street life
  • Evening: Belgrade nightlife – bars or clubs, vibrant and unforgettable

Trip Plan 2: Central Serbia (Tradition + Landscapes)

Day 1 – Royal Heritage
  • Morning: Topola & Oplenac – royal church, vineyards, and Serbian history
  • Afternoon: Wine tasting in Šumadija – local varieties and countryside views
  • Evening: Traditional dinner – slow food and village hospitality
Day 2 – Mountains & Monasteries
  • Morning: Studenica Monastery – UNESCO heritage and spiritual calm
  • Afternoon: Žiča Monastery – medieval art and historic coronation site
  • Evening: Mountain lodge stay – fresh air and quiet nights
Day 3 – Nature Escape
  • Morning: Tara National Park – forests, viewpoints, and hiking trails
  • Afternoon: Drina River viewpoints – emerald waters and dramatic landscapes
  • Evening: Sunset over the mountains – peaceful and grounding

Trip Plan 3: Eastern & Southern Serbia (Slow Travel)

Day 1–2 – Natural Wonders
  • Morning: Đerdap National Park – Iron Gates gorge and Danube views
  • Afternoon: Golubac Fortress – medieval towers above the river
  • Evening: Dinner by the Danube – calm waters and quiet reflections
Day 3 – Thermal & Cultural Serbia
  • Morning: Niš Fortress – Roman roots and historic crossroads
  • Afternoon: Skull Tower – powerful history and remembrance
  • Evening: Local food in Niš – grilled specialties and southern flavors
Day 4–5 – Villages & Nature
  • Morning: Sokobanja – spa town, nature walks, and fresh mountain air
  • Afternoon: Rtanj Mountain – hiking and legendary landscapes
  • Evening: Slow evenings – sunsets, silence, and starry skies
