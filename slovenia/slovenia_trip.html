Trip Plan 1: Ljubljana & History (City + Culture)

Day 1 – Historic Heart of Ljubljana
  • Morning: Ljubljana Castle – panoramic city views, medieval architecture, and funicular ride
  • Afternoon: Old Town Walk – Tromostovje (Triple Bridge), Preseren Square, riverside cafés
  • Evening: Traditional Slovenian dinner – try potica or kranjska klobasa, relax along the Ljubljanica River
Day 2 – Museums & Art
  • Morning: National Gallery of Slovenia – classic Slovenian art
  • Afternoon: Museum of Contemporary Art – modern vibes and local exhibitions
  • Evening: Evening stroll through Metelkova – vibrant street art and music scene
Day 3 – City Reflections & Food
  • Morning: Central Market & Dragon Bridge – fresh produce and iconic landmarks
  • Afternoon: Ljubljanica River Cruise – calm, scenic, perfect for photos
  • Evening: Coffee at a riverside café – soak in the city vibe

Trip Plan 2: Lakes & Mountains (Bled + Bohinj + Triglav)

Day 1 – Bled & Surroundings
  • Morning: Lake Bled – rowboat to Bled Island, visit Church of the Assumption
  • Afternoon: Bled Castle – medieval architecture and stunning lake views
  • Evening: Try kremsnita – enjoy dessert with a view
Day 2 – Bohinj & Nature
  • Morning: Lake Bohinj – swimming, kayaking, or lakeside walk
  • Afternoon: Savica Waterfall – short hike to this beautiful cascade
  • Evening: Dinner in Bohinj Valley – local trout specialties
Day 3 – Triglav National Park
  • Morning: Vogel Cable Car – panoramic views of the Julian Alps
  • Afternoon: Hiking trails or scenic photography spots
  • Evening: Relax in a mountain lodge – enjoy fresh air and Slovenian cuisine

Trip Plan 3: Coastal & Karst Slovenia (Adria + Caves)

Day 1 – Piran & Slovenian Coast
  • Morning: Piran Old Town – Tartini Square, narrow streets, seaside vibes
  • Afternoon: Piran Harbor & Walls – climb for Adriatic sunset views
  • Evening: Seafood dinner – fresh Adriatic fish at a local restaurant
Day 2 – Karst & Caves
  • Morning: Škocjan Caves – UNESCO-listed underground marvels
  • Afternoon: Postojna Cave – train ride inside the limestone wonder
  • Evening: Local wine tasting – Karst wines and light dinner
Day 3 – Coastal Nature & Relaxation
  • Morning: Sečovlje Salt Pans – nature walks and birdwatching
  • Afternoon: Strunjan Nature Park – coastal cliffs and salt marshes
  • Evening: Sunset along the Adriatic – end the trip with coastal serenity
