Trip Plan 1: Classic Paris & Landmarks (3 Days)

Day 1 – Paris Essentials:
  •  Morning: Eiffel Tower – take the lift to the top for panoramic views
  •  Afternoon: Arc de Triomphe & Champs-Élysées stroll
  •  Evening: Seine River Cruise to see the city lights
Day 2 – Culture & History:
  •  Morning: Louvre Museum – explore the Mona Lisa and ancient art
  •  Afternoon: Notre-Dame Cathedral & Sainte-Chapelle
  •  Evening: Montmartre – visit Sacré-Cœur and enjoy the street artists
Day 3 – Relax & Food
  •  Morning: Breakfast at a local boulangerie (croissants & baguette)
  •  Afternoon: Musée d'Orsay or Orangerie Museum
  •  Evening: Try a classic French dinner with a cheese platter and crème brûlée

Trip Plan 2: French Castles & Gardens (3 Days)

Day 1 – Loire Valley Arrival:
  •  Morning: Travel to Château de Villandry
  •  Afternoon: Explore the stunning Renaissance gardens
  •  Evening: Walk around the local town and have dinner
Day 2 – Castles & Wine:
  •  Morning: Château de Chambord – admire the architecture and history
  •  Afternoon: Château de Chenonceau – cross the bridge over the river
  •  Evening: Wine tasting at a nearby vineyard
Day 3 – History & Monuments:
  •  Morning: Memorial Charles de Gaulle – learn about France’s modern history
  •  Afternoon: Free time for local cafés and small towns
  •  Evening: Return to Paris or local accommodation

Trip Plan 3: Food & French Culture Tour (3 Days)

Day 1 – Parisian Food Experience:
  •  Morning: Baguette & croissant breakfast at a boulangerie
  •  Afternoon: Food market tour – try local cheeses, macarons, and pastries
  •  Evening: Dinner at a traditional French bistro
Day 2 – Iconic Desserts & Cafés:
  •  Morning: Visit a patisserie for Paris-Brest and crème brûlée
  •  Afternoon: Stop by famous cafés in Saint-Germain-des-Prés
  •  Evening: Relax at Luxembourg Gardens
Day 3 – Sweet & Savory Finale:
  •  Morning: Cooking or pastry class (learn to make macarons or croissants)
  •  Afternoon: Cheese platter tasting at a specialty shop
  •  Evening: Sunset view from Eiffel Tower & farewell dinner
