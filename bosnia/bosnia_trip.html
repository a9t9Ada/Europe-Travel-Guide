Trip Plan 1: Sarajevo City Vibes (History + Culture)

Day 1 – Historic Heart of Sarajevo
  •  Morning: Baščaršija Old Bazaar – wander the cobblestone streets, discover Sebilj Fountain, and feel centuries of Ottoman and Austro-Hungarian charm
  •  Afternoon: Gazi Husrev-beg Mosque – admire intricate architecture, peaceful courtyards, and the spiritual soul of the city
  •  Evening: Traditional ćevapi for dinner – savor authentic Bosnian flavors while the city lights shimmer along the Miljacka
Day 2 – Stories of the Past
  •  Morning: Tunnel of Hope Museum – explore resilience and history hidden beneath the city
  •  Afternoon: Yellow or White Fortress – climb for breathtaking sunset views over Sarajevo
  •  Evening: Modern Bosnian dining – contemporary flavors in a cozy, historic setting
Day 3 – Relax & Food Day 3 – Hills & Reflection
  • Morning: Cable car to Mount Trebević – panoramic city views and fresh mountain air
  •  Afternoon: War Childhood Museum – poignant stories and reflective exhibits
  •  Evening: Coffee along the Miljacka River – slow stroll, city lights, and timeless charm

Trip Plan 2: Mostar & Herzegovina (Stone, Sun & Water)

Day 1 – Old Town Charm
  •  Morning: Stari Most (Old Bridge) – iconic, scenic, and perfect for photos
  •  Afternoon: Kujundžiluk Street – cobblestone alleys, artisan shops, and the heart of Mostar’s heritage
  •  Evening: Koski Mehmed Pasha Mosque viewpoint – golden hour over the Neretva, peaceful and cinematic
Day 2 – River & History
  •  Morning: Blagaj Tekke & Buna River – serene waters, centuries-old monastery nestled in cliffs
  •  Afternoon: Počitelj Medieval Village – stone streets, towers, and timeless vistas
Day 3 – Nature & Flavors
  •  Morning: Kravice Waterfalls – turquoise cascades perfect for photos and relaxation
  •  Evening: Dinner by the Neretva River – fresh fish or grilled specialties, sunset included
Optional Day – Wine & Pilgrimage
  •  Morning/Afternoon: Herzegovina wine routes or visit Međugorje – local flavors and spiritual vibes

Trip Plan 3: Nature Escape (Slow Travel)

Day 1–2 – Lakes & Waterfalls
  •  Morning: Jajce Waterfall – stunning, cascading centerpiece of the town
  •  Afternoon: Pliva Lakes & Wooden Watermills – fairy-tale scenery, nature walks, and photography
  •  Evening: Local Flavors by the Lake – try fresh trout or traditional pastries at a lakeside café, soaking in the serenity
Day 3 – History & Culture
  •  Morning: Travnik Fortress – panoramic views of a historic Ottoman town
  •  Afternoon: Ivo Andrić’s Birth House – literary history and cultural insights
  •  Evening Travnik Old Town – wander through bazaars, admire traditional architecture, and stop for coffee in a local café
Day 4–5 – National Parks & Adventure
  •  Morning: Una National Park – rivers, forests, and fresh mountain air
  •  Afternoon: Štrbački Buk Waterfall – dramatic cascades and peaceful trails
  •  Evening: Adventure & Sunset – try kayaking, rafting, or zip-lining; end the day with a sunset view over the river
