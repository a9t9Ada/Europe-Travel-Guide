Trip Plan 1: Classic Rome & Landmarks (3 Days)

Day 1 – Ancient Rome Essentials:
  • Morning: Colosseum – explore the iconic amphitheater and its underground chambers
  • Afternoon: Roman Forum & Palatine Hill – walk through the heart of ancient Rome
  • Evening: Piazza Venezia & Capitoline Hill – enjoy the city lights and historic views
Day 2 – Vatican & Baroque Rome:
  • Morning: St. Peter’s Basilica – marvel at Renaissance art and climb the dome
  • Afternoon: Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel – admire masterpieces by Michelangelo and Raphael
  • Evening: Castel Sant’Angelo – stroll along the Tiber and enjoy riverside views
Romantic Squares & Local Life:
  • Morning: Trevi Fountain – toss a coin and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere
  • Afternoon: Spanish Steps & Piazza di Spagna – relax, people-watch, and take photos
  • Evening: Piazza Navona – dinner at a local trattoria and enjoy gelato

Trip Plan 2: Northern Italy & Culture Tour (3 Days)

Day 1 – Milan Arrival:
  • Morning: Arrive in Milan and check into accommodation
  • Afternoon: Explore the stunning Renaissance gardens
  • Evening: Walk around the local town and have dinner
Day 2 – Venetian Charm:
  • Morning: Rialto Bridge & Grand Canal – gondola ride and photo ops
  • Afternoon: St. Mark’s Square & Basilica – admire Byzantine architecture
  • Evening: Enjoy a Venetian seafood dinner and explore canals by night
Day 3 – Florence & Renaissance Art:
  • Morning: Florence Cathedral (Duomo) – climb the dome for panoramic city views
  • Afternoon: Uffizi Gallery – see works by Botticelli, Leonardo, and Michelangelo
  • Evening: Ponte Vecchio – sunset walk and dinner near the river

Trip Plan 3: Italian Food & Culture Tour (3 Days)

Day 1 – Rome Culinary Experience:
  • Morning: Classic Italian breakfast – cappuccino and fresh cornetti
  • Afternoon: Food market tour – try cheeses, olives, and fresh pasta
  • Evening: Dinner at a traditional Roman trattoria
Day 2 – Iconic Italian Dishes & Cafés:
  • Morning: Learn to make Pasta alla Carbonara or fresh pizza in a cooking class
  • Afternoon: Visit a historic café for tiramisu and espresso
  • Evening: Walk around Trastevere – enjoy local atmosphere and aperitivo
Day 3 – Sweet & Savory Finale:
  • Morning: Visit a gelateria – taste artisanal gelato flavors
  • Afternoon: Street food tasting – Sicilian pizza, arancini, or cannoli
  • Evening: Sunset view from Piazzale Michelangelo (Florence) or Janiculum Hill (Rome) & farewell dinner
